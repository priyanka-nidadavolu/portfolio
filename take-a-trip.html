OBJECTIVE OF OUR PROJECT

To develop an intuitive and efficient travel planning platform using Django, Python, MySQL, and HTML/CSS that offers curated travel suggestions and connects users with travel agents. The platform aims to streamline the planning process through robust backend management, secure data storage, and a responsive user interface, ensuring a seamless and personalized experience for users.

Concepts Used

Mathematical programming- providing best and cheapest solution
Data science - providing travel support bot
Technical skills- Django for preparing frontend and backend
